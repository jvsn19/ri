Tale of the Gronklewonkler

A downloadable Hashtag Altgame for Windows, macOS, and Linux

Download NowName your own price

In this game you play as a mythical creature known only as the Gronklewonkler.

As the Gronklewonkler you will use WASD to move and the mouse to look. Running can be achieved by holding down the shift key. If you wish to devour a cat, you may click on it. You can hit escape to leave at any time and your progress will be saved. To complete a level you must jump into the white yellow and pink "portal".

There are four levels and two possible endings.

In the first level you must fall off the platform and onto the one far below.

Made in less then a week for a bit of stress relief, and to think about weird game design.
