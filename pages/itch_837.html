Succulents

Tend to an abandoned garden deep underground.


The original jam game was made in less than 48 Hours for Nordic Game Jam 2016.

FEATURES

  • Recreated the growing system. Now you actually water the soil!
  • Added an additional plant that hadn't made it into the jam build (more to come).
  • Added the Bag of Seeds, and Seeds. Take control over where plants grow!
  • Added the Shovel. Dig up unwanted plants.
  • Added Toggle Controls. Simply click to pick up/drop objects. If you prefer holding, simply hold. :)
  • The game now saves on exit. Return to your garden!
  • Fullscreen (won't work properly for non 16:9 resolutions). Press Alt+Enter to toggle.

FIXES

  • Fixed the initial water level in the well. It now starts full.
  • Tidied up the collisions for waterdrops/bucket.
  • Fixed how the music behaves for the plants. Volume will now decrease when plants wither and die.
  • Fixed the infinite water source glitch from tilting the bucket too far.
  • Fish no longer swims in the air.
  • Tweaked the border graphics and background. Will do a visual overhaul in a future update.
  • Various edge-case fixes.
