Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“As a small team, we want to put all our efforts into crafting something special. The time, feedback and support that early access provides is invaluable to ensuring that we can build something you'll love as much as we do.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“It is hard to estimate how long the game will remain in early access, probably on the order of 12-24 months. Ultimately, we aim to ensure that the final, released version of the game is a cohesive and polished experience.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“
  • Complete Story Arc: Unravel the history and secrets of Lamplight Station and it's residents.
  • Deep Tech Trees: Craft your way up multiple tech trees to build and create complex systems. Use nuclear fusion to keep yourself warm, create assembly lines to build lithium-air batteries, or use gene splicing to make super foods.
  • Explore miles of space station: Each new area of the station will come with it's own challenges: heat, radiation, low gravity and hard vacuum are just a few of the obstacles you'll need to overcome to uncover the secrets of Lamplight Station.
  • Design and build complex machines and factories: Need more solar panels? Create your own assembly lines to turn raw materials into complex parts and much more.
  • Leave Your Mark on the World: Turn the station into a dystopian hellscape - or a green, lush oasis. The choice is up to you.
”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“
  • Basic survival mechanics are in game (temperature, health, hunger and clothing).
  • Basic enemy AI, including some stealth mechanics.
  • Fully Destructible environment.
  • Basic building mechanics - gather and place blocks to create safehouses, buildings, stairs and other structures.
”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We'd like to keep the price fair, and in relation to the amount of content, polish and enjoyment that the game can offer. In all likelihood, the price will go up a little upon release.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Clear and open communication is important, and your feedback will be vital to creating a game we're proud of. We plan to create as many avenues for feedback and dialogue with our early adopters as possible so we can hear your thoughts and suggestions, and respond to questions. We plan on sharing regular updates on our progress through the steam platform.”

Devblog #10 Water, Water Nowhere

The endgame for the tech tree in Lamplight Station involves the ability to build a lot of complex and high-tech machinery. Things like stellarators, gene splicers and molecular printers are on the horizon. Most of these machines will require power, cooling and item inputs. This also allows for a lot of creativity, because the player can decide how to lay out these structures optimally, and automating them can become a possibility.

You have to walk before you can run, so some of the first multiblock structures that the player can craft will be much simpler.

Fog Collectors

These allow for the collection of moisture from the air, and built using two parts: A water barrel, and one or more collector blocks.


The collectors can be stacked vertically over a single barrel, at a slight efficiency penalty. Water can be collected from the barrels by the player for other uses.



Tier-II water barrels will be able to pipe water elsewhere for use in irrigation, cooling, etc.



Composter

Water is primarily useful for growing plants. It will be a challenge to even get started growing plants in such a harsh environment. Dirt is not available and must be created from ingredients scrounged from the level. Biomatter can be inserted into another craftable machine: the Improvised Composter, which will output key ingredients for creating soil.



As more machines become available during the development of the game, the possibilities for combining and using them creatively expands.

Devblog #9 Level Up!

Last week, we focused on improving the level design. There's much more variety in terms of tiles that spawn in the world now. The level also received some tweaks to make it easier to travel up/down in the level and increase the verticality a bit.



Highrise offices will be a good place to find rare items, such as wood, used in flooring only the most luxurious areas.



Terraced 'apartments' can provide a quick escape from the cold, and a way to travel up or down in the station.



Various storefronts can contain food items, or electronic components.



Stairways provide quick access to some of the upper levels of the station.



These industrial cooling towers actually house tenement housing in the large cavities within.



Skywalks connect some of the more well-to-do housing and office units.

About This Game

Lamplight Station is a story driven exploration-crafting game. Lost on massive space station you must use your wits as you build, craft and fight for survival.
The game features a fully destructible environment and a deep crafting system, with an emphasis on player creativity and choice. You'll need to overcome radiation, cold, heat and the station's less-friendly inhabitants in order to survive.

Features:
  • Destructible Environment
  • Deep Science-based crafting
  • Build Complex Machines
  • Story-Driven Atmospheric exploration
  • Fully simulated life support systems


Hard Scifi Cyberpunk Setting

The technology you can craft and use in Lamplight is based as much as possible on what is actually theoretically possible. You won’t find any magical midichlorians here.

Never Enough Machines

Design and build complex assembly lines and machines to create the technology you need to survive. You’ll need to rely on your own creativity to build systems that work.

Your Future Is In The Past

Scrounge through the ruins of a ruined society to uncover their secrets, and the scraps you need to survive.

Story-Driven Atmosphere

As you play, you’ll uncover the deeper stories behind Lamplight Station, it’s purpose, and the lives of it’s former (and current) inhabitants.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows 10
    • Processor: i-7 2.8GB
    • Memory: 16 GB RAM
    • Graphics: GeForce GTX 1050
    • Storage: 8 GB available space
    • Additional Notes: Less powerful systems specs will likely be supported once we can fully test the game on a variety of systems.
