Jet Sweep 505

A downloadable game for Windows and macOS

JET.SWEEP.505

Welcome in the Huntsland Navy penal fleet, convict! Your job is to help clear the world's largest and most dangerous minefield. Do not ask us why. Those are orders from above!

Take control of the latest in mine sweeping technology and obliterate sea mines, wave by wave!


About the Game

This game has 8 levels in total and takes about 40~50 minutes to complete.

Controls

You can play this game with either keyboard and mouse or an X-Box controller.

The controls require some getting used to. Use your keyboard (WASD) or the controller's analogue stick to point into the direction you want your ship to go: The ship will then steer towards that direction.

Tips

  • Getting rid of moored mines gives you more space to outmaneuver incoming mines: Try to get as many with your torpedoes as you can.
  • Before and between waves, there will be some dialogue which you can skip. New enemies will not spawn until they have finished talking, however. You can use this extra time to take care of moored mines!
  • You can also shoot mines that are still out of sight
  • Don't collect health pick-ups when you don't need them, yet: They may come in handy later on!


This game has been my spare time project for about a year now. And while it's far from perfect I have learned a lot along the way!

Hope you enjoy!
