It's hard to beat a hard to beat game, and Sometimes is just that (Probably only because I was REALLY bad at making games when I made it...) A relatively simple platformer with fun gameplay and a mysterious atmosphere.

Sometimes is unfinished, however I am no longer developing it. I am proud of it but I am also unhappy with it to an extent. I personally enjoy playing it and enjoyed making it immensely. At some point I might make one last update adding in one or two levels and changing the GUI slightly.

Features:

  • 15 levels.
  • Timer. Fastest times are shown on the level select screen.
  • Unlockable character customization.
  • Lava.
  • More lava.
  • Water too.
  • X-Box 360 controller support.
  • Simple easily understood graphics.
  • Frustration
