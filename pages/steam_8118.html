Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We feel that there is a good opportunity to gain community feedback and help shape the way Golf With Your Friends is developed.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We initially stated "the end of 2016" but we under estimated the time needed.
We think around mid to late 2018 should be enough time to complete and release our fully featured game. This may change overtime.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Below are the features we are aiming to add to the game, the final product may differ from our original plans.

  • 7 Levels ✔
  • Level editor
  • Multiple game modes
  • Full ball customization (Hats, Trails and Skins)
  • Offline single and Hot-seat modes
”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“This early access version currently contains:
  • Full 12 player multiplayer
  • Modifiers (low gravity, different ball shapes, etc)
  • Game modes (Hoops and Hockey)
  • Private rooms
  • 7 levels containing 18 holes each (126 holes total)
  • customisation (Hats and Trails)
”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We plan to raise the price once we leave early access.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“The biggest reason for entering early access is the opportunity to gain community feedback.
We already have a plan and know what we want the finished product to be. But we also want to hear from you, what would you like to see in the game? What do you dislike?

We want to work with the community to make the best game that we can.”

Getting there.

Hey everyone
Yes, its another boring news post.

We are still hammering away at the level editor, obviously we would have wanted it out months ago but building an editor from scratch does take time.
However not much longer ;)
As stated in the last update we have most of the foundation built for the editor there’s just a few annoying glitches and things to add.

Here’s a terrible video I threw together tonight to show 5 holes being made in the editor (yes they are bad) also excuse my video editing skills :)

Assets
A lot of themes are now in the process of receiving even more foliage, course pieces, Etc Which again takes a very long time, (model asset, sculpt details, bake said details, paint asset, make prefab in unity) the whole process takes quite a while and we would much rather build everything in house so you get a unique experience worth your money instead of buying hundreds of assets that dont match and every other game uses.



Once I feel the 6 themes have a substantial amount of assets to work with I will be working on new themes, so please let me know what you’d like to see. And yes space is on the list :P

About This Game


Golf With Your Friends is an entertaining, challenging, multiplayer mini golf game for up to 12 players.
The game currently has 7 levels containing 18 holes each.

The controls are simple and the game is designed for the user to be able to host or join a game within a few seconds.
The game will be hosted on our servers where the user has the ability to add a password if so required or leave it open to the public.

Key Features

  • 7 Vibrant challenging levels containing 18 holes each, 126 holes in total.
  • 12 Player multiplayer
  • Ball customization (Hats and Trails)
  • Modifiers (low gravity, different ball shapes, etc)
  • Gamemodes (Hoops and Hockey)
  • Player created servers
  • Steam invite
