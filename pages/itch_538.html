A Night in Piedmont High

A downloadable game for Windows


It's 9 o'clock. Do you know where your children are?

Survive a night in Piedmont High, where strange things are said to be happening. Tortured souls wander the halls, fleshy mounds form in the cracks, and students are suddenly going missing...

A Night in Piedmont High is a test to see if I can replicate both the tension and charm of classic survival horror games like Silent Hill and Alone in The Dark.

This is my first demo release, including 6 small rooms in one area of the school. Many things are placeholders now, but I'd love for you to give it a shot and let me know what you think.


Controls (controller recommended):

WASD, Arrow Keys, left stick or d-pad - Move

Shift, B Button - Run

Ctrl, RB - Aim

Space, A Button - Interact (shoot when aimed)

Alt, R, X button - Reload

?, Q, Y Button - Heal

Tab, Start - HUD/Map

Esc, Select - Quit (when HUD is up)
