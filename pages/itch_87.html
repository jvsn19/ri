v0.41 - Some inventory and UX stuff

MewnBase Â» Devlog
180 days ago by Cairn4 (@cairn4)
Share this post:

New gameplay hazard! Items that the player drops on the ground run the risk of "blowing away" during sandstorms. So consider keeping your valuables in a Cargo Container ;)  Check out the video below.

HUD: new clickable Map button. Also updated the Flashlight button so that it's easier to see on dark backgrounds. Both now have mouse-hover tool tips that display their keyboard shortcuts.

New base module: Added Water Spigot that lets you retrieve water from your base's water supply. Kinda old-timey looking at the moment, like a well pump, but think it at least gets the point across, will probably give its art another pass later at some point.

Updated player "eat" animation. Nom nom.

Inventory: When dropping items, hold SHIFT to drop the entire stack instead of dropping just 1 at a time.

When transferring items to and from a Cargo Container, hold Shift when clicking to transfer the whole stack. Single click without shift will transfer just 1 item at a time.
