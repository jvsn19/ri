Winters Brew

A downloadable game for Windows, macOS, and Linux

A game made for a Kris Kringle game jam I organized for a collective of young Irish game developers I'm a part of.

Collect the ingredients for a reinvigorating potion and make it home for the winter festival.

Controls:

  • Left click to interact and progress in story segments
  • W,A,S,D to Move
  • Mouse to look
