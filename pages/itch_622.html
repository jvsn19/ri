Vilmonic is an artificial life simulator sandbox game that takes place in a lonely distant future. You are the last caretaker of the final few life forms on the planet. Protect each animatroid and fungolite species, analyze their DNA, selectively breed new species, craft tools and buildings, dig rivers and make new islands, fend off the mindless haywire zombitons, dig up ancient tech, and maybe, possibly, uncover a staircase leading down into ancient infested underground laboratories...
