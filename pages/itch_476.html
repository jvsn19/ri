AGAB [Prototype]

The journey of a green slime that would like to be red. A three button platformer. (Note: this is just a prototype built for https://itch.io/jam/slime-studio-jam)

Goals

Squish and sqloosh yourself to the right side of the screen, collecting red tomatoes along the way! Try to collect 'em all, if that's what you'd like.

Controls

Right arrow to jump right,
Left arrow to jump left,
Up arrow to jump up,
Oh and you can double jump too!
