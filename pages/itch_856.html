Qudong

A downloadable game for Windows, macOS, and Linux

Qudong is a physics-based, two-player, pong-inspired game with relaxing atmosphere and fast gameplay. You gain a point, when your opponent misses the ball.


Controls

Qudong requires controllers to play.
Point of collision determines the angle of the ball. Pressing the trigger results in a stronger bounce.


Tips
Press S to share one controller with your friend.

Press Select on controller to alternatively control bounce strength with your right joystick. (RECOMMENDED)
Hold Start to reset the game.
