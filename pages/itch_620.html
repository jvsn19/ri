Vilmonic Lite

Support This Game

Vilmonic is an artificial life simulator and evolution sandbox game that takes place in a lonely distant future. You are the last caretaker of the final few life forms on the planet. Protect each animatroid and fungolite species, analyze their DNA, selectively breed new species, craft tools and buildings, dig rivers and make new islands, find ancient tech, and fend off the mindless haywire zombitons...

Craft Farm Breed Evolve!

The  full version  has many more features, like toxic waste to speed up mutation rates, ancient junk & treasure, many more crafting recipes for roads and beverages and food and more!

how do i breed plants? i didnt know i could do that!

Reply
bludgeonsoft360 days ago

All of the life forms in Vilmonic reproduce asexually, so to create different breeds you will need to propagate those that have the traits you are looking for. To breed yellow fungs you will have to pick the yellowest of your green fungs and plant them around, continually picking and planting the yellowest of each generation until you finally have yellow fungs. This is essentially artificial selection. You can use a similar technique to generate any color fung. Their colors migrate based on the colors of the rainbow, so red becomes orange becomes yellow - green - blue - purple (indigo) - and back to red.
