Go, Go ,Radzilla!

Go, Go, Radzilla! is a simple game about destroying a city with your radioactive powers. It is played with two buttons (Z and X).

This game was my first ever entry to Ludum Dare (#34), under the theme "Two Button Controls" and in less than 48h.

Controls
Z - Jump
Z (on air) - Stomp
X - Fire
X ( hold) - Charge Fire
