    About the game

    Join Nikolaï Orelov in his search for redemption as you journey through an iconic, propaganda art style Russia in 2.5D gameplay. Survive the aftermath of October Revolution & travel accross Russia. Experience the thrill of being a stealthy Assassin in an exclusive modern setting.

    Highlights

    • Survive the aftermath of October Revolution & travel accross a changing Russia.Dive into the middle of a major historical event served by iconic propaganda art style and its revolutionary symbolism. Start in Ekaterinburg and escape Ipatiev house with Duchess Anastasia. Jump on the famous trans-Siberian train towards Kazan to meet Leon Trotsky. Infiltrate the Kremlin and then escape from Moscow while being chased by a tank !
    • Experience the thrill of being a stealthy Assassin in an exclusive modern setting.Explore a brand new cotemporary setting in the 20th century, with its modern architecture and interactive environmental stealth systems, such as elevators, radios & trip wires. Interact with new vehicles like trucks, trains & even tanks. Avoid searchlights & snipers. Hijack telephones to draw enemies away from their patrols. Use modern weapons to electrocute & poison guards.
    • Switch between two playable characters: use Nikolaï & Anastasia’s unique playstyles to overcome specific challenges.Nikolaï the brutal but efficient. Embody Nikolaï a superlative marksman, snipe targets and detonate mines from a distance with his customized scoped and silenced rifle or get up close with its bayonet. Discover Nikolaï’s Mechanical Winch, a versatile tool that sends electric shocks, disrupt electrical components or electrocutes enemies in key locations.

    System Requirements

    • Minimum for PC:

    • Operating System: Windows 7 SP1 or Windows 8 or Windows 8.1 (32/64bit versions)
    • Processor: Intel Core 2 Duo E8200 @ 2.6 GHz or AMD Athlon II X2 240 @ 2.8 GHz
    • Memory: 2GB
    • Graphics: nVidia GeForce GTS450 or AMD Radeon HD5770 (1024MB VRAM with Shader Model 5.0)
    • DirectX: 10
    • Hard Drive: 4GB

    • Recommended for PC:

    • Operating System: Windows 7 SP1 or Windows 8 or Windows 8.1 (32/64bit versions)
    • Processor: Intel Core i3 2105 @ 3.1 GHz or AMD Phenom II X4 955 @ 3.2 GHz
    • Memory: 4GB
    • Graphics: nVidia GeForce GTX 470 or AMD Radeon HD5870 or better (1024MB VRAM with Shader Model 5.0)
    • DirectX: 11
    • Hard Drive: 10GB
