About This Content

This is the official soundtrack to The Keep: an old-school dungeon crawler with real-time combat. Featuring 14 songs from the PC/Mac version of the game - all composed & produced by Martin Linda. All Songs are in MP3 Format.

The soundtrack includes the following songs:

01. The Keep - Main Theme (2:01)
02. The Keep - Intro (1:27)
03. The Keep - Theme 2 (2:10)
04. The Keep - Theme 3 (2:53)
05. The Keep - Theme 4 (2:09)
06. The Keep - Theme 5 (3:09)
07. The Keep - Theme 6 (1:40)
08. The Keep - Theme 7 (3:00)
09. The Keep - Theme 8 (3:07)
10. The Keep - Theme 9 (2:00)
11. The Keep - Theme 10 (2:24)
12. The Keep - Theme 11 (3:12)
13. The Keep - Final Fight (1:10)
14. The Keep - Outro (2:18)

The Soundtrack will be placed in your The Keep folder in the Steam Directory: …Steam\SteamApps\common\The Keep\Soundtrack
