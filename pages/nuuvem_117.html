    About the game

    You're Rock Gunar, hire mercenary. Quick trigger finger and clear battle tactics are the order of the day as you take on this ultimate survival mode challenge to finish off all the aliens on this ship!

    You will die (often) But pure brute force, equipment enhancement and weaponry will help you knock down all these worms, one round at a time!

    Highlights

    • Upgrade and customize your weaponry as you learn from your mistakes and discover how to bring down the aliening hordes!
    • Use the FRENESI mode to cause further damage.
    • Drop waves of various alien enemies and face severe odds against terrible alien bosses in the final level confrontation!
    • Reboot the matches with your weaponry unlocked in New Game + mode for a more immersive, bloody and absurdly way!
    • Show off your fitness and gloat with friends as you climb to the top ranking!
    • Choose songs on your BOOMBOX to help focus on the pace of the task and prepare unique mixtapes as you progress!
