    About the game

    Borderlands is a game with a first person perspective, but relatively different from the other titles of the genre FPS, because it has several elements of RPG. Developed by Gearbox Software, which is also responsible for the Brothers in Arms franchise games, the game puts you in charge of several different classes, each with its own characteristics.

    All in all, there are three different classes: Soldier, Hunter (Hunter) and Siren (Mermaid). Stand out for the Soldiers, who have an interesting advantage. Whoever opts for these soldiers will not have to worry about acquiring ammunition. The Soldiers count the Digital Backpack, a kind of backpack in which it is possible to obtain physical items carrying only digital data.

    In addition, it is possible to get your characters to gain new skills, which are acquired as the player progresses through the game. Some of these capabilities extend recharging time, defense and more. But nothing compares to the greatness of the Borderlands arsenal.

    The game has a system capable of supplying about 500,000 different types of weapons. To identify each of them, simply zoom in and check attributes that appear on the screen. Through the appearance you can also see if you are viewing a good rifle or not. Weapons with larger barrels are more accurate, while colors demonstrate the special properties of each of them (incendiary, corrosive or electric). The ammunition comb and the scope can also be seen without difficulty.

    Highlights

    • Character Development: Choose from four different characters, each with your skill set. As your character develops in the story, you'll be able to choose your ability to suit your playing style!
    • Art Style: Borderlands' artistic style combines traditional rendering techniques with hand-drawn textures to give this FPS a solid and attractive look.
    • Obsession by Weapons: Choose from literally hundreds of thousands of weapons, each with its manufacturer, specifications and advantages. The revolutionary content generation system ensures that you will find an almost infinite amount of destruction tools to match your style of play.
