Just wanted to post an 3D diorama that I just completed using Sprytile! These are the Undersea Friendos, three friends in search of adventure and soggy sandwiches.

If you'd like to see a WIP and the Sketchfab scene, check it out on Cartrdge.

November is the last month that donations will be open for Sprytile! Work will continue on the addon but I will not be able to take donations from December onwards. If you enjoy the work I'm creating with Sprytile or if Sprytile has helped you with your own 3D work, please consider making a donation this month your support really helps!

If you're not in a position to donate, I completely understand. Sharing art you made with Sprytile on social media or leaving an honest review of Sprytile on itch.io are other great ways to support the project!

Thank you, and have fun creating with Sprytile!
