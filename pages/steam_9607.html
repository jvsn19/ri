Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“As developers, we feel that having active community participation in the creation of the game is necessary for Animyst to become everything that it can. We are pushing the envelope on what a small team can accomplish in delivering exciting PVP gameplay on high population game servers and having active community feedback will help the game be better on release. We also need to see how players use the tools we give them so as to better balance the game.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“1 Year.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The final version will feature a wealth of extra content including: spells, crafting recipes, clothing items and building pieces. The diversity of offensive and defensive magics will quickly grow during development as we interface with our player-base and evolve the combat system. Eventually we will implement systems for players to move between game zone servers, adding city centers and a cross server item storage system. We plan on adding support for more languages and and a system that will automatically translate what other players say into the players native language.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The game is set on a single, giant open world map for players to explore and compete on. Environmental resources function as weapons and ingredients in higher tier crafting recipes. There are dozens of spells, craftables, and clothing items to customize your character with. Players may also quickly construct stone ruins to function as tactical fortifications. The highest tier of craftables requires the blood of other players, best gathered through engaging in PVP. There is also a unique and simple local speaking system instead of a traditional map chat.
Animyst is a highly optimized multiplayer experience that is built to run on most players gaming PCs, including lower-tier gaming laptops.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The price will remain the same.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Animyst is made by a tiny development team of two people. We feel the project truly began as the two of us modded other survival genre games in order to try and host the perfect PVP experience. To help the community thrive on our modded servers, we created a dedicated Discord app channel for players to meet and interact. We are taking a similar approach, and will be maintaining a Discord channel dedicated to player feedback, FAQ's, bug reporting, and most importantly, the channel will provide a space for players to hang out and talk with each other. We plan to use this alongside the Steam Community page to ensure that players are easily able to find information that they need. The two of us will be available to talk with the community and will set definite hours that players can reach us with problems or feedback.
We are so excited to hear what the community thinks of our game. We are introducing some extremely different and innovative systems of player versus player gameplay and we need feedback so that we could better know what works and what could be improved.
We plan on releasing gameplay updates most weeks and may push frequent small updates for bug fixes and PVP balancing changes in the beginning months.”

About This Game

Immerse yourself in an open-world PVP sandbox set in a vast ancient desert full of magic and secrets. Gather ingredients from nature and combine them to make your own personalized load-out of spells and armor. Build ruins to help you hold territory and kill other players for their gear. There are no rounds or matches, just a perpetual world to explore and dominate.

-Perpetual open world.
-Craft powerful spells from gathered ingredients.
-”Reduced-Rage” Partial Full-Loot System: Players drop only equipped items on death.
-No classes. No champions or heroes. Freely customize your character's abilities.
-Easy and unique local speaking system.
-No quests. No hints. Explore and discover in a world where knowledge is power.
-Craft and place towering stone structures and ruins.
-Gear up and get ready to fight quickly, without a grind.

Come experience the future of PVP, drawing from the best in battle royale and persistent world survival games. This game will be receiving numerous frequent updates to add more content including spells, building pieces, clothing items, as well as, many gameplay enhancing changes and visual updates.
