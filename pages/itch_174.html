Description:
------------------------
The Sky House is a small metroidvania about a flying creature in a flying house. 
The game is inspired by REDDER and VVVVVV and take 30 mins to complete.

Hope you will like the game.

------------------------
Controls:
------------------------
Arrows => Move
X => Jump
X X => Fly
