Dungeon Deathball (demo)


In the cruellest corners of the dankest dungeon, the fearsome Mad Lads gather their slaves. And in their raucous rampage, they challenge the weak to a bloodsport most brutal...


The game? Deathball.

The reason? Barbaric entertainment.

The prize, should the slaves survive? Their freedom!

 


 

Dungeon Deathball is a compact, tactical ball sport with Rogue-like elements.

It's quick to pick up and play, casual enough for players who aren't hardcore fans of tactical games, but gives a decent challenge so that you're always one step away from becoming floor wax.


Features

  • Simple turn-based tactics on a grid.
  • Pick up & play: rounds that can be finished in a few minutes.
  • 'Rogue-like' elements: unique, randomised challenges with every game and when your players die they're gone forever.
  • 6 stages of unique challenge: deadly rules are layered in with each new round. Can you survive until the grisly end?


Note - Work in Progress!

Consider this to be an early access version which I'm giving away for free (for now).

Many features and improvements still to come. Please share your thoughts and let me know what you'd like to see in the game!
