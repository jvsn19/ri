Dead Land

A downloadable game for Windows, macOS, and Linux

Travel between the land of the dead and the land of the living to solve problems in this first person point 'n click game.

You have the unique ability to phase between the land of the living and the land of the dead. Unfortunately you are stuck in this valley. Use your powers to help the locals and get out of here.

This was made in 72 hours for the ludum dare 30 jam.
