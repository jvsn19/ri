Color and Shapes

A downloadable game for Android

A very simple shape-and-color matching activity that I made for my 2-year-old, where you drag the right shapes on the right of the screen to the slots on the left side.

No ads, no upsell screens, no easily-pressable buttons that take you out of the game. Just shape matching :)

Created using libGDX. Source code will be available soon on github!
