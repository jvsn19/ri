Seedlings -V0.35

A downloadable game for Windows

Seedlings is a puzzle platformer where you use a seed to control different plant creatures, each with unique abilities to try continue the story.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Works with Keyboard/Mouse or Controller (Controller recommended).
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Still in early development. All art, animation, music and coding is done by me. Coded in Gamemaker 2.0
