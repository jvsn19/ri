About This Game

Rocket Island is a fun 3D educational video game set in an open world environment. Rocket Island provides a pleasant environment to learn about science, technology, sustainability, water cycles, community participation, and mathematical thinking skills.

Featured Pedagogy includes situated learning, repeated use of artefacts, 35,000 words throughout gameplay (130 page book), developing strategic planning and mathematical thinking skills, and self-directed learning.

Features 25 characters to learn from and work with to collect resources to launch the rocket to space! Player can work hard to become an astronaut!
