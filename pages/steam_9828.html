About This Content

Includes the 'Tactical Kit' outfit which enables Lara to craft special ammunition with fewer resources, a 'Carbon Fiber' Compound bow skin, and the rare 'Bow Master' foil card, which gives Lara all bow upgrades, increased damage, and zooms further.
