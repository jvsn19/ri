Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We are mainly doing Early Access to get feedback from the Community on hole design and interface to make the game play as smoothly and fun as possible.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We are expecting to exit Early Access sometime in early 2019. We have gone through Early Access once before with Contraption Maker, so we have a good sense of how long it will take to bring Minigolf Blast to full release.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Based on Community feedback will we be modifying some of the current holes. Full release of the game will have Steam Controller support. We will be adding Steam Achievements and Stats. We are exploring VR support, but can't promise it as this point.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The ball physics is currently 100% done and rock solid. There are four different game types right now (normal, race, safari, and safari-race). There are 6 18-hole courses and one (Tutorial) 9 hole course. So 117 total holes currently. 42 different golf balls with various super powers.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The price may rise when we exit Early Access.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“The plan is to include a feedback button in the Early Access of Minigolf Blast so that the Community can give feedback immediately right in the game if they want to.”

About This Game

Minigolf Blast is a fun, whimsical, online multi-player, hot-seat multiplayer, and single player minigolf game. Easy to play. Lots of variety with 117 holes on 6 different courses.

  • Six 18 hole course and one 9 hole course (117 total holes)
  • Four different game types
  • Online Multiplayer
  • Many different ball super powers
  • Leaderboards
  • Rock solid ball physics

Being designed and coded by the creator of Sierra's 3D Ultra Minigolf, The Incredible Machine, and many other Dynamix/Sierra games.

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • OS: Windows® Vista
    • Processor: 1.8 GHz Processor
    • Memory: 4 GB RAM
    • Graphics: 512MB dedicated VRAM
    • Storage: 180 MB available space
