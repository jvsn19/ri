A pink monster is stuck in a box that tries to kill him. But every box leads to more boxes that try to kill him even more than the previous box. This continues for 40 boxes... maybe. If you like hard platformers then this is for you. The replayability comes in getting faster times for each world.

Features:

- Good controls
- Good level design
- Good soundrack (credited below)
- Several colors
- Very difficult
- Fun for most
