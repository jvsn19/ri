Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“WARNING: This isn’t stage that meant to be fun and game yet!. For now and next 3 - 5 months this application are going to be a “testing ground” for a “tester” and “pre-player” who interested to deal with frustration, lots of bug, painfully bad gameplay with unfinished contents and unplayable mechanics. before it’s becoming “play ground” after months of development.

so ****MAKE SURE**** to download and play at your risk as you will! we will have no responsibility for being a bad game! unless you are well prepared and expected and still here to help sending critics and comments for the sake of the final stage development.

As a small indie team with no budget at all and got tons of bill to pay. Can’t help but with love and persistent for video games we eager to created Free-To-Play game called Black_Faith a story driven psychological/survival horror/adventure game based on local horror stories podcast.

The goals is to developing game while able to fully listening, exploring and crying for help from the love and support of a video game community. To gain fans and funds from peoples that interesting and has thought to talk and share with us. All this will benefits both party for the better result of development. so fans and devs are win win!

If you satisfied with this unfinished-product and interested to see it’s possible potential with much more better quality content and features such as new gameplay mechanics, new map locations, story campaigns and anything “YOU” request! With your support, any amount will keep us breathing and keep this game going and bring this vision to life. To fulfil it’s destiny, Not only will our team be able to invest more time, Also allowing us to please the beloved gaming community. We can’t made it out alive without your pure passion for video games. Thanks for playing “Black_Faith.”

“You are now the part of us, to be tester and savior! help us do this together! Send your feedback, suggestion and your vision””

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“3 up to 5 months.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“• Story Campaign (up to 3 Episodes)
• Full free-roam mode
• Many potential plan that we are not sure to share, yet it’s possible!”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Black_Faith. v.0.3.2

This version is the earlier stage of the game (Pre-Alpha), will only have one game mode “free roam” which is the “testing ground” for trying out all the basic mechanics we had now and to developing more contents to create “playing ground” in near futures
Features
Open-world/free-roam in the large scale of mystery forest
Menu and settings”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Although this is free to play game but not gonna lie. there is going to be DLC that required real life money to access certain hard-work contents in the future but with no force and no need to pay to enjoy. For now the free contents are valuable enough to play around for a life-times (this business model is much like what “Fortnite” does )”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Everyone who willing to be Tester and Pre-Players or Supporters are free to shoot any questions and suggest/request yours ideas via our steam community forum and facebook page and discord server

Remember your critics are the most important thing for us, we really need it that much.”

BlackFaith
