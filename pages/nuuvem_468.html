    About the game

    A song echoes... awakening much more than a new adventure! Face an obscure journey through the bowels of the classic 2D platform; while zombies, skeletons, traps and lots of rock'n'roll fill your adventure!

    The iconic and legendary partnership between Dumativa and Castro Brothers once again invades the world of Pixel Art! The Legend of the Dead is part of the acclaimed saga Songs for a Hero, featuring addictive mechanics, classic 8 and 16-bit ambiance, plus the famous dynamic soundtrack of the series (and an occasional deadly boomerangs ¯ \ _ (ツ) _ / ¯);

    Survive all the mysteries that the night of the dead holds for you!

    Highlights

    • Vocals by none other than the Son of the Heavy Metal God himself, DETONATOR
    • Fight your way through 3 new levels with haunted mansions and crypts full of dark creatures;
    • Classical 8 and 16-bit environments;
    • A brand-new campaign with plenty of rock'n'roll;
    • Zombies, skeletons, traps and lots of metal fill your journey!
