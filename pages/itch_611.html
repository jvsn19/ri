This next version includes a pretty big change to animatroids and their brains, so please start with a new world! In this version you will find it a bit harder to keep your anims from going extinct, especially early in the game and in small or medium worlds. Also, anims are now much more susceptible to overhydration, and they now lay eggs!

If you play this beta version, let me know how you like the new balancing. Feel free to post any bugs you find here in the comments, or post a report here: http://bludgeonsoft.org/report-a-bug/
