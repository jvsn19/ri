Here are some of Tiny Platformer's features, which you can learn about by reading the source code:

  • Platforming with ledge grabs, wall jumps, moving platforms
  • Freeform grappling-hook mechanic
  • Character animation and animated-trail rendering
  • Particle effects
  • Dynamic music: mute/unmute channels for different segments
  • Generates and stores animated (fire) sprites from code
  • Big ol' bossfight as a finale
