    Faq
    Can I switch between Humble Rewards and Charity?
    Yes, you can switch begin putting 5% towards Charity or 5% towards Humble Rewards at any time. Just visit this page or make the adjustment in the checkout before completing your transaction.
    Do I get Humble Rewards for gift purchase?
    Yes, any purchases made while logged into your account and opted into Humble Rewards will result in the appropriate amount of Wallet credit being added to your account.
    Can I use Humble Rewards towards Monthly or Bundle purchases?
    No, Humble Rewards (and the Wallet credit that you earn) can only be spent on items purchased in the Humble Store.
    Do my Humble Rewards expire?
    No, your Humble Rewards will remain in your wallet. You can make a choice whether or not to spend your credit with every Humble Store purchase you make.
